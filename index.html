NCT or Neo Culture Technology, a K-POP boy group, is based in South Korea.They are under the label SM Entertainment and have members from South Korea, Japan, China, America, and Canada. They debuted in 2016 with the sub-unit NCT U. NCT's main concept is trying new sounds and styles, moving forward and over all being "Neo", which also is why they will continue to add new members through the years.

Since their debut they have released a total of seven full length albums, nineteen "mini albums" or EP's, and fourty-five singles. Every NCT song has it's own unique sound and aesthetic, none ever sound exactly the same. They keep their fans entertained by not only the new sounds but as well with their astounding music videos, which not only seems to fit the song just right but also fits the members just right as well.

SM Ent. was made by Lee SooMan, who was also was a singer for some time in the 1970's and early 1980's. He founded SM Ent. in 1989.
Some other extremely successful groups in SM Ent. include Red Velvet, Shinee, EXO, Super Junior, TVXQ, Girls Generation and many more.
